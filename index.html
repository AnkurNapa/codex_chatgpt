About Craft Beer School

Instruction rooted in science, culture, and the joy of sharing a pint.

Craft Beer School is an interactive online platform dedicated to educating and empowering the next generation of beer professionals and passionate enthusiasts. Founded by seasoned experts with real-world experience in brewing, beer science, beer communication, and branding—including podcasting and storytelling—our school offers a dynamic space to gain industry-relevant skills, share insights, and connect with fellow brewers and mentors.

From grain to glass, our curriculum covers the science, artistry, and business of beer. Whether you're refining your craft, starting a venture, or exploring brewing as a serious passion, you'll gain practical knowledge and hands-on techniques rooted in industry standards.
